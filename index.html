ACCOMMODATIONS 

Coco Grove Beach Resort in Talahid, Almeria, Biliran, offers rustic beachside cottages designed for simplicity and relaxation. These native-style accommodations, typically made with bamboo and nipa, provide a cozy ambiance for a tranquil getaway. Each cottage is equipped with basic amenities such as beds, fans, and a small veranda, allowing guests to enjoy the natural beauty and ocean breeze. Due to limited availability and varying prices depending on the season, it's recommended to contact the resort directly for details on specific accommodations and rates.

Located along the western coast of Biliran Island, it's a popular destination for those seeking a peaceful getaway near the sea, away from crowded tourist spots.
